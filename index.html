What is Cyberium?

Cyberium is low code platform to build information and value exchange applications for your internal or external organization.

See our website or build powerful applications on our cloud service!

What is Cyberium Studio?

Cyberium studio is cloud and blockchain orchestrator for building, managing and executing applications which connect various blockchains and applications using classical APIs and JSON RPC APIs for blockchain.
General users can manage their cryptocurrencies from the wallets along with the apps. Designers and engineers can build apps to enhance the commercial applications of cryptos using the Studio operation window.
You can build public as well as non public apps. Public apps have user interfaces or api interfaces for general public to interact with your app. Non-public apps can be your own internal processes and workflows with manual or automated process steps.

See our resources for getting started!

What is Cyberium Wallet?

Cyberium wallet holds cryptocurrency. The keys are managed by the platform. The cryptos can be exchanged on platform or outside platform to external addresses.

See our resources for getting started!

What is Cyberium App?

You can build public facing apps in Studio. Calendar app is such an example where anyone can use the app to manage their public calendar and engagements. Anyone can create their own version of Calendar app for their own custom needs like process steps, automation, inetegration and branding etc.
You can build apps which can interact with cryptocurrency of your userbase as well. The studio platform makes it easy to manage your apps, functions and value together on a single unified platform.

See our resources for getting started!

What types of blockchains are available on Cyberium?

We support 2 types of blockchains on our platform. Namely, Ethereum and Hyperledger Fabric. You can host your contracts/chaincodes and integrate with workflows for end to end blockchain operation.
More blockchains are comming soon and announcements for early beta users or partners will be published as appropriately in social media and newsletters.

See our resources for getting started!

How can I use it?

User: Cyberium is free for app and wallet users. You can interact with apps and cryptocurrencies based on your choices and needs.

Host: Cyberium is available either as a cloud portal, or as enterprise on-premise installation that you can be customised according to your needs.
